# Product Brief — Wellness Tracker

**App summary.** A comprehensive daily wellness PWA covering morning/evening check-ins, task management, time tracking, budget monitoring, and growth habit logging — all in one place.

**Target user.** Chase — a person managing health, productivity, and finances who needs one tool that handles the full daily loop without switching between apps.

**Main pain.** Tracking sleep, mood, tasks, time, spending, and habits across separate tools creates friction and gaps; the data never tells a coherent daily story.

**Core value.** One localStorage blob, one Supabase sync, one daily ritual — everything Chase tracks lives together so patterns are visible and the day has a clear start and end.

**V1 scope (shipped at v15.10):**
- Morning/evening check-in form (sleep, mood, energy, supplements, meds, notes)
- Task management with ideas sub-tab
- Time tracking with scripture streak counter
- Budget tool with wants tracker
- 90-day history with analytics, AI summary, and export
- Growth habit logging with streaks and wins log
