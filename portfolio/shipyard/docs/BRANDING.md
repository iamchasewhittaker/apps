## Voice

Shipyard talks like a first mate reading the log — status first, no decoration, plain nautical metaphors only when they clarify (never when they obscure).

## Logo direction

Ship's helm: 8 spokes, 4 cardinal handles (rectangular), 4 intermediate steel dots (`#4A90DE`), brass rim (`#D7AA3A`), deep sea background. Rendered in `LogoIcon.tsx`. Nautical theme label toggle available in-app (Projects/Ships, Dashboard/Fleet, etc.).
