# Clarity iOS Logo Mockups - Rationale (V1)

## Family System Applied
- Shared dark navy rounded-square tile with subtle depth to match the YNAB Clarity visual language.
- High-contrast white symbol language for iOS homescreen readability.
- Blue verification badge used selectively (Check-in, Budget) per your guidance.
- Accent colors reflect each app's functional role while keeping family cohesion.

## Per-App Rationale
- **Check-in:** Pulse waveform inside a wellness ring to communicate daily health check flow; includes blue badge to suggest successful completion.
- **Triage:** Funnel + stacked bars communicates sorting and prioritization under capacity constraints; amber cue reflects decision urgency.
- **Time:** Circular session ring with clock hands signals timed focus sessions and streak discipline.
- **Budget:** Envelope/ledger hybrid mark reflects planned allocation and tracking; badge reinforces confidence/verified-plan feeling.
- **Growth:** Rising line with a small seedling cue expresses long-term progress and personal development.
