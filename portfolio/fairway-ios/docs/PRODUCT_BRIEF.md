# Product Brief — Fairway

**App summary.** Fairway is an iOS lawn management OS for Chase's 4-zone Rachio irrigation system in Vineyard, UT — zones, heads, problem areas, schedules, fertilizer plan, soil data, spreader calculator, and shrub bed upkeep in one place.

**Target user.** Chase — homeowner with a Rachio controller, Utah clay soil, and a grounds-crew standard for his own lawn.

**Main pain.** Lawn care knowledge lives in Chase's head, Rachio's app, fertilizer bag labels, and a soil test PDF — no single place to see the full picture or track what was applied and when.

**Core value.** One app that holds every zone, every product, every schedule, and every soil reading — so Chase runs his lawn like a professional, not from memory.

**V1 scope.**
- 4 zone cards with status dots; per-zone detail with heads, problem areas, schedule, and Beds (Zone 1)
- Fertilizer season plan (6 applications, date windows, product links) + spreader calculator (lbs, bags, refills, dial setting)
- Soil test view (13 nutrients, bar chart, April 2026 readings)
- Maintenance task list, season test checklist, and mow log
- Product + equipment inventory with stock log and service history
