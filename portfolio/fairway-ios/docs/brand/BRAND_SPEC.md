# Fairway — Brand Identity Project

## What This Is
Fairway is a home lawn care app. The core concept: help homeowners achieve course-quality lawn results. The name is intentional — it references a golf fairway, and the branding leans into Masters tournament aesthetics (Augusta National green, gold, azaleas, retro sunset landscape).

## Approved Logo
**File:** `fairway_minimal.html`

The final approved mark is a minimalist retro sunset circle badge:
- Deep Augusta green background (`#0c1e10`)
- Glowing sun (three concentric circles, gold/amber tones)
- Three rolling fairway bands in descending greens
- Single gold flag pin
- Light italic Cormorant Garamond wordmark in gold (`#d4af37`)
- One thin gold outer ring

**Three colorways:**
1. Dark (primary) — dark green bg, gold wordmark
2. Cream — parchment bg, dark green wordmark
3. App icon — rounded square, same as dark

- **Supporting:** General Sans (UI), Playfair Display (alternate display)

## Onboarding Flow (5 screens)
1. **Splash** — Logo mark, tagline "Course-quality lawn care, engineered for your yard"
2. **Zones** — Top-down lawn map, connect Rachio, zone layout
3. **Schedule** — Weekly watering schedule view, Rachio connected indicator
4. **Soil** — Soil health bars (N/P/K/pH), fertilizer recommendation card
5. **Get Started** — CTA + Sign In

## Design Principles
- Minimalist — every element earns its place
- Masters-adjacent — refined, traditional, premium without being stuffy
- Retro warmth — sunset palette, organic curves, not sterile tech
- Never: purple gradients, rounded sans wordmarks, generic green
