# Clarity Command — Branding

## Identity

**Name:** Clarity Command  
**Tagline:** Daily accountability. Rooted in faith. Built for your family.  
**Feel:** Military briefing meets morning devotional. Clean, direct, no fluff. This app means business.

## Palette

| Token | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| `bg` | `#0a0d14` | App canvas — darker than other Clarity apps, heavier weight |
| `surface` | `#111520` | Cards, modals |
| `surfaceHigh` | `#181e2e` | Inputs, elevated surfaces |
| `border` | `#1e2638` | Dividers, card borders |
| `text` | `#f3f4f6` | Primary text |
| `muted` | `#6b7280` | Secondary text, labels |
| `accent` | `#c8a84b` | Gold — covenant, purpose, identity. Primary CTAs. |
| `accentLight` | `#221d0e` | Gold tint background |
| `green` | `#3d9970` | Met targets, streaks, success |
| `greenLight` | `#0d2b1e` | Green tint background |
| `red` | `#e05050` | Missed targets, urgency counter, conviction |
| `redLight` | `#2b0f0f` | Red tint background |
| `yellow` | `#e0b030` | Partial completion |
| `blue` | `#4a7fc0` | Stats, informational |

**Why gold:** Gold evokes covenant, consecration, and purpose — central LDS themes. It's also distinct from the teal/green used in Wellness and other Clarity apps, giving Command its own visual weight.

## Icon Concept

Shield + compass hybrid — protection of family + direction/purpose. Dark background with gold accent lines. Should feel like a mission patch.

## Tone

Direct and convicting. Never gentle when targets are missed. Never shaming when targets are met. The voice is the voice of someone who loves you enough to tell you the truth — like a bishop, a coach, and your wife all in one.

Examples:
- ✅ Met: "All targets met today. This is what showing up looks like. Do it again tomorrow."
- ❌ Missed: "You applied to 0 jobs yesterday. 'You are wasting time.' Anxiously engaged means TODAY. (D&C 58:27)"
