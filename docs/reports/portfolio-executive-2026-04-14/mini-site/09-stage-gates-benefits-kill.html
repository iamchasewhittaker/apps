Stage Gates, Benefits Tracking, and Kill Criteria

Decision discipline for what to launch, scale, pause, merge, or retire.

Stage Gates

  • G1: Strategy fit
  • G2: Build readiness
  • G3: Launch readiness
  • G4: Post-launch review

Benefits Tracking

  • Value hypothesis per initiative
  • Baseline and target KPI ranges
  • Owner + review cadence

Kill Criteria

  • Low strategic fit
  • No traction after runway
  • Maintenance cost exceeds value

Recommendation

  • Require stage-gate completion for all externally shipped work.
  • Tie benefits tracking to CFO and CPO review cycles.
  • Apply kill criteria quarterly to active and archived sets.
