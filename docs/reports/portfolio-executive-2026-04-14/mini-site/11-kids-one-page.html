Portfolio Report (Kids One-Page Version)

Simple language, quick read, presentation-ready.

What is happening?

The company made lots of apps. That is great. But some apps now do similar jobs, so things feel crowded and confusing.

What is going well

  • Building fast
  • Trying useful ideas
  • Shipping often

What needs fixing

  • Too much overlap
  • Some docs do not match
  • Need stronger data safety checks

Simple plan

  • Keep the best apps
  • Combine apps that do the same thing
  • Retire old apps clearly
  • Use one quality checklist before launch

Timeline

  • 2 weeks: clean app list and fix docs
  • 30 days: start combining overlap and market top app
  • 90 days: fewer duplicate apps, better quality

Bottom line

The team is strong. The next step is to focus, simplify, and grow the best apps.
