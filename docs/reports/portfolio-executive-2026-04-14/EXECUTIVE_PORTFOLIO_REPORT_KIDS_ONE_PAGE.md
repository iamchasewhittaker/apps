# Portfolio Report (Kids One-Page Version)

Date: 2026-04-14

## What is happening?

This company made lots of apps.  
That is awesome.

But now some apps do the same job.  
That makes things confusing and harder to manage.

## What are we really good at?

- Building fast
- Trying new ideas
- Making useful tools

## What needs to get better?

- Too many similar apps
- Some instructions do not match
- Need stronger safety checks for data
- Need a clearer story for customers

## Simple plan

### Keep the best apps

- Job Search HQ
- Clarity apps
- YNAB money tools

### Combine overlap

- Put similar apps together when they do the same thing

### Stop old work

- Mark old apps as retired and do not keep working on them

## What leaders should do now

1. Pick top 3 product groups
2. Decide: keep, combine, pause, or retire each app
3. Add one safety + quality checklist for all app launches
4. Add one simple dashboard to track progress
5. Meet monthly and assign clear owners

## Timeline

- **2 weeks:** clean app list + fix docs  
- **30 days:** start combining overlap + launch best app marketing  
- **90 days:** show fewer duplicate apps + better quality

## Bottom line

The team is strong.  
Now the goal is to **focus**, **simplify**, and **grow the best apps**.
